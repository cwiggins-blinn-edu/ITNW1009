Course & Certification Overview

A single, offline-ready courseware player covering Days 1–12 of ITNW 1009 and the four official AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02) exam domains.

Domain 1: Cloud Concepts — 24%

Value proposition, deployment/service models, Well-Architected Framework. Covered Days 1, 2, 11.

Domain 2: Security & Compliance — 30%

Shared Responsibility Model, IAM, security services, monitoring/audit tooling. Covered Days 3, 4, 8, 12.

Domain 3: Cloud Technology & Services — 34%

Compute, containers, storage, networking, databases, DNS/CDN. Covered Days 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10.

Domain 4: Billing, Pricing & Support — 12%

CapEx vs. OpEx, Pricing Calculator, Support Plans, cost management tools.
