Course Overview

This course builds the knowledge and hands-on lab experience needed for two AWS certifications: the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02) and the AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01). Over five weeks you'll move from core cloud concepts, security, and the shared responsibility model, through compute, storage, networking, and databases, into AI/ML fundamentals and generative AI on AWS — finishing each certification track with a full timed practice exam.
